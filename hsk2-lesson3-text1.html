🎧 Listening — 听力 (10 Points)
📍 At the apartment entrance — wife notices husband came home late.
📍 A wife and husband at the apartment entrance. He is returning from work late.
👩
今天回来得有点儿晚啊！
Jīntiān huílái de yǒudiǎnr wǎn a!
You came home a little late today!
👨
今天公司事情很多，下班以后还开了一个会。
Jīntiān gōngsī shìqing hěn duō, xiàbān yǐhòu hái kāile yí ge huì.
There was a lot of work today, and we had a meeting after work.
👩
晚饭已经做好了，你先洗手吧。
Wǎnfàn yǐjīng zuò hǎo le, nǐ xiān xǐshǒu ba.
Dinner is already ready. Wash your hands first.
👨
好的，我先喝点儿水，再去吃饭。
Hǎo de, wǒ xiān hē diǎnr shuǐ, zài qù chīfàn.
Okay. I will drink some water first and then eat.
