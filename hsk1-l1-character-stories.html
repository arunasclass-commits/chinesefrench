HSK 1 · 第一课 你好 · the picture draws itself, then becomes the character

By shape is what your eye sees in the modern character — it helps you write it. By origin is what the character actually depicted 3,000 years ago — it helps you understand it. They often disagree, because brush simplification reshaped the strokes. Both are shown rather than pretending one is the other. Each card is also tagged with what kind of character it is, so you know when there is a real story and when there simply isn't one.
Only characters made of separable parts can be built this way. 一、五、八、大、我 are single units — nothing to drag — so they're left out. Drag each part into the right box in the 田字格.
